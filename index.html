About Us

Who We Are?

We are a vibrant community dedicated to the exploration and celebration of Hinduism.

What We Do?

We host debates, events and much more related to hinduism.

Why We Do It?

There is no common platform currently where people around the world can properly discuss or debate about hinduism. This has caused misinformation related to hinduism to grow significantly. We aim to educate people through healthy discussions and debates.
